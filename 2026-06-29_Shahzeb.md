### 3. Bug Fix — Sign-Out Routing to Wrong Port

**File:** `src/stores/auth.js`

**Problem:** Clicking Sign Out was routing to `http://localhost:4000/app` instead of `/login`.

**Root Cause:** `logout()` was `async` and awaited `api.post('/auth/logout')` before clearing the token. Since no component awaited `auth.logout()`, `router.push('/login')` ran while the token was still set. The router guard (`meta.guest && isLoggedIn → redirect to Home`) bounced the user back to `/app`.

**Fix:** Made `logout()` synchronous — clears token/user state immediately, fires the server-side token revocation in the background (fire-and-forget).
